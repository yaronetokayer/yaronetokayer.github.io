Welcome to my interim website. I am a doctoral candidate in the Department of Physics at Yale University studying cosmology and galactic dynamics under advisor Frank van den Bosch. I also work with Priyamvada Natarajan and Michael Koss. My work leverages computational tools, data analysis, and pencil-and-paper analytical work to better understand cosmology.

My website is currently under construction - a new flashy not-just-HTML one with my research projects and interests is coming soon. Meanwhile:

    • Reconciling gravitational lensing measurements with cosmological models
    • Quantifying the bias in X-ray surveys of supermassive black holes
